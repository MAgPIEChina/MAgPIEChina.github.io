About Me


Hao Cai is currently a PhD. candidate, at Department of Agricultural Economics and Management, Zhejiang University,China, and a visting researcher at Environmental Change Institute, the University of Oxford, UK. He holds a BSc and a MSc in Management Science and Enginering.

He is a member of MAgPIE-China research group. has published peer-review articles in Nature Sustainability, Nature Food, Environmental Impact Assessment Review, etc.
